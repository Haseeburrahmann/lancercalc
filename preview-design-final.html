How It Works
How we calculate your freelance tax
01

Self-employment tax

Social Security (12.4%) applies up to the $176,100 wage base. Medicare (2.9%) has no cap. An extra 0.9% Medicare applies above $200K. Net income is multiplied by 92.35% first â€” then rates apply. You can deduct 50% of SE tax from your AGI.

02

Federal income tax

Applied to your adjusted gross income using the latest 2025 IRS brackets after the standard deduction ($15,000 single / $30,000 married), your SE tax deduction, and any business deductions you enter.

03

State income tax

Applied based on your selected state's flat or effective rate. Nine states charge zero income tax. California and Oregon are among the highest. Select your state above for the accurate calculation.
