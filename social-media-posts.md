## Tips for Posting

1. **Timing:** Post Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM EST for max engagement
2. **Engagement:** Reply to EVERY comment within 2 hours
3. **Don't be spammy:** Space posts 5-7 days apart per group
4. **Add value first:** Answer questions in the group for a week before posting your link
5. **Target groups with 10K+ members** for meaningful traffic
6. **Use the question hook** ‚Äî posts that start with a question get 2-3x engagement
7. **Follow group rules** ‚Äî some groups have dedicated self-promo threads
8. **Track which posts perform** ‚Äî double down on what works
